Experience

Since 1982, we have specialized in the field of legal videography. Our experience encompasses every aspect of legal video including Depositions and CMEs to complex "A Day in the Life" videos and more. We are able to bring your case to life through the use of professionally produced video.

Certification

We are nationally certified to meet your video production needs. The Certified Legal Video Specialist (CLVS) designation through the National Court Reporters Association (NCRA) is the ultimate certification for a legal videographer. We are long-standing members of the NCRA and Florida Court Reporters Association (FCRA).

Dedication

Our focus over the past 39 years has been in the legal arena. We have filmed many thousands of Video Depositions, Comprehensive Medical Examinations, Site Inspections and more for courtroom use and settlement purposes.
