Additional/Supplementary Video Services

  • Remote Video Depositions:

    With our extensive video production education, background and legal video experience, we are knowledgeable in the field of Remote Video Depositions. It may be enticing to press the Zoom record button, but the NCRA warns: “If the Zoom meeting unexpectedly shuts down at any time, any recordings of the meeting could be irretrievably lost.” In addition, Zoom does not offer time and date stamping of their meetings.

  • Video Deposition Editing:

    Our professional digital editing ensures precise deletions of unwanted material such as objections and also allows for expert insertion of material, as needed, such as language translation and more.

  • Video-Text Synchronization:

    We provide VTS upon request – Synchronization of the text of the transcript with the video. With the use of courtroom presentation software such as Sanction and Trial Director, a Video-Text Synchronized deposition can be edited even on the fly in the courtroom during trial.
